Travel

Austin, Texas

I moved to Austin Texas in 2012 to study psychology at the University of Texas. Hook 'Em!

Sydney, Australia

UT had a student swap program that allowed me to Study Abroad at the University of New South Wales for the spring semester in 2015. Truly one of the coolest experiences of my life!

Palma, Mallorca

I moved to Mallorca in the fall of 2016 and spent a year teaching abroad. It was by far the most beautiful place I will ever live. There were mountains to hike and beaches to explore. The pueblos were amazing.

Madrd, Spain

I decided to move to Madrid and continue teaching in an attempt to improve my spanish. In Mallorca they spoke Mallorquin which was a dialect of Catalan. I was able to greatly improve my spanish and spent 3 years in Madrid loving every second of it!
